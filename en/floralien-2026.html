Floraliën Ghent 2026
— Stay & free ticket

Carlton Hotel is an official partner of Floraliën Ghent 2026. Book your stay and receive a free entrance ticket to the world-renowned flower festival.

What's included?

  • 1 night at Carlton Hotel Ghent
  • 1 free entrance ticket to Floraliën Ghent 2026 (worth €27)
  • Entry via a pre-assigned time slot — for a relaxed, optimal visit
  • Our usual personal service and delicious breakfast

How to claim your ticket

  1. Book your stay directly through our website
  2. Mention "Floraliën" in the comments field of your booking
  3. We'll confirm your ticket by email
  4. Collect your voucher at reception during check-in

Practical info

📅Floraliën: 1–10 May 2026, daily 8 AM to 8 PM
📍Location: Floraliënhal, Ghent
🎟️One ticket per booking, while supplies last
🛏️Valid on new bookings via our website
