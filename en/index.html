About us

Small hotel. Big welcome.

With 22 rooms, we are deliberately not a big hotel. You will notice it in everything: in the personal welcome, the insider tips we share about the city, and a breakfast that keeps surprising our guests.

We are a two-minute walk from Ghent's main station. Close enough for easy arrival, far enough from the bustle for a good night's sleep.

Jeffrey and his brother Steven have been running the Carlton since 2012. No hotel chain, no anonymous director on the seventh floor. Just two brothers who believe a hotel can be personal.

Sustainability

Our commitment to a greener planet

At Carlton Hotel we are committed to sustainability and are proud of our partnership with Go Forest. Thanks to this initiative, we invest 20% of the revenue from our hotel parking in planting trees in Peru.

This way we support reforestation, offset the carbon emissions of our parking guests, strengthen biodiversity and contribute to the fight against climate change.

Carlton Hotel Gent is certified with the Green Key label. This means we take responsibility for nature and the environment, without you having to compromise on comfort.

Go Forest

20% of parking revenue goes towards reforestation in Peru. So far 1,917 trees have been planted, absorbing over 1,121 tonnes of CO₂. See our impact report.

Green Key

Internationally recognised eco-label for sustainable hotels, awarded by the Foundation for Environmental Education. Certified since 2021. Learn more at greenkey.global.

Ghent

The city, for those who weren't sure what to do

Graslei & Korenlei

The historic quays along the Leie river. Beautiful during the day, even more so in the evening. Ten minutes by tram, or a pleasant walk through the city.

Gravensteen

A medieval castle in the middle of the city. Solid, real and not tarted up for tourists. The tour is worth it.

Saint Bavo's Cathedral

The Ghent Altarpiece by Van Eyck hangs here. One of the most famous paintings in the world, within walking distance of your room. Worth it.

Vrijdagmarkt

Friday and Saturday: a real market on a real square. No tourist folklore, just Ghent at its best.

STAM & SMAK

The City Museum for those who want to understand the history, SMAK for contemporary art enthusiasts, or the simply curious.

Citadelpark

Within walking distance of the hotel. Perfect to decompress when the city centre gets a bit much, or for a morning run.

More inspiration? Visit Gent has it all, or just ask at reception.

Good to know

Reception & check-in

Check-in is from 15:00 to 22:00. Arriving later? Please contact us in advance. At check-in you'll receive a free city map and guide.

Free luggage storage before arrival and on your check-out day.

Parking & EV Charging

Our private garage offers space for your car at €18 per night. Reserve in advance, availability is limited.

Charge your car while you enjoy a good night's rest? That's possible! Use your own charging card at our 5 charging stations. The rate is €0.56 per kWh (max 22 kWh, type 2 connector).

Getting to the city centre

Trams 1 and 3 stop right in front of the hotel and take you to the heart of Ghent in a few minutes. Or walk, it's a pleasant 20 to 25 minute stroll through the city.

Ghent-Sint-Pieters station is a two-minute walk away. Train from Brussels takes around 30 minutes.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I check in early?

Yes, free of charge and subject to availability. Let us know when you expect to arrive, we'll do our best to have the room ready. If it's not, we'll store your luggage and move it to the room as soon as it's free.

Can I check out late?

Almost always. Unless the hotel is fully booked and the room is urgently needed. The absolute latest is 1pm, and it's free. Just ask.

Is breakfast included?

Not by default, unless you booked it that way. But you don't need to reserve it in advance either. Just come down when you're hungry, we'll add it to your bill at check-out. Monday to Friday 7am-10am, weekends 8am-11am.

Extra bed or cot?

An extra bed is available at €25 per night, except in the Standard Triple and Deluxe Double. A baby cot is free and fits in every room. Let us know in advance and everything will be ready on arrival.

How can I pay?

Bancontact, Visa, Mastercard, iDEAL and cash. American Express is also possible, but via a payment request by email. A small detour, but it works.

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation until 3pm the day before arrival. Booked a non-refundable rate? That rate is cheaper, but cancellation is not possible.

Can I smoke?

Inside: not permitted. Outside: there is a smoking area at the hotel entrance.

How do I reserve a parking spot?

By email, phone or WhatsApp, do it in advance. We only have 5 spots and they fill up quickly.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately not. To ensure the comfort of all our guests, pets are not permitted in the hotel.

Arriving by car?

Ghent has a low emission zone (LEZ). Foreign vehicles must be registered in advance to avoid a fine. Our hotel and the route to and from the motorway are outside the LEZ, but if you drive into the city centre you will enter it.
