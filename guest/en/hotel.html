The hotel

Everything you need to know for a carefree stay.

Check-out is possible until 11:00 AM. Need a bit more time? Let us know at reception and we'll be happy to look into the options. Late check-out is almost always possible, at the very latest until 1:00 PM.

Early departure? If you're leaving before 8 AM, please let us know the evening before. We'll make sure everything is ready for you.

A pleasant atmosphere starts with a few simple guidelines.

  • No smoking: Smoking is not permitted inside the hotel. There is a designated smoking area at the entrance. A cleaning fee of €150 will be charged in case of violation.
  • Quiet hours: We ask for quiet between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM, both in the rooms and in the hallways.
  • Registered guests: Only registered guests have access to the rooms. This is for everyone's safety.
  • Maximum occupancy: Each room has a maximum number of guests based on its size and safety regulations.
  • Fire safety: All rooms are equipped with smoke detectors. Open flames, such as candles, are not permitted.
  • Pets: Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in our hotel.

Fast and free Wi-Fi in every room and in all common areas. You'll receive the password at check-in, or simply ask at reception.

Arrived early or leaving late? No problem, we'll store your luggage free of charge at reception until 10:00 PM. If your room isn't ready yet, we'll bring your luggage up as soon as it becomes available.

Your room is cleaned daily and all toiletries are replenished. Prefer not to be disturbed? Just let reception know or hang the sign on your door.

At Carlton, hospitality goes hand in hand with respect for people and the environment.

  • Go Forest: 20% of the revenue from our hotel parking goes towards reforestation in Peru. So far, more than 1,900 trees have been planted.
  • Green Key: Carlton Hotel Gent is certified with the Green Key label, an internationally recognised standard for sustainable hotels.
  • Local: We work with regional suppliers as much as possible. Jam from Callas, free-range eggs, bacon from Breydel in the Ghent region.
  • Ecological: Our cleaning and maintenance products meet the highest ecological standards.

Curious about the impact? Check out our Go Forest report.
